Privacy & ZKID

Zero-Knowledge Identity (ZKID) track

Zero-Knowledge Proofs (ZK) enable proving without leaking: you can prove a statement is true without revealing the underlying data. In Web3 this reshapes the balance between transparency and privacy.

ChainMind is designed with privacy in mind. Future versions could use ZK to:

  • Prove “my balance is above X” for a payment rule without revealing the exact balance.
  • Prove compliance or eligibility without exposing full transaction history.

This page lets you mask sensitive data in the UI as a first step toward privacy-aware behaviour.

Mask sensitive data in UI

When enabled, addresses and amounts are partially hidden across Dashboard and Payments (e.g. 0x1234…abcd). Data is only masked in the browser; the server still has full values.

Masking is off.
